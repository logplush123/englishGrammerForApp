Voice Transformation (Active to Passive)

We will explore the transformation of active sentences into passive voice across five categories.

  1. Tense
  2. Modal Verbs
  3. Let
  4. Imperative
  5. Infinitive

In active sentences, the structure typically follows:

Subject + Main Verb (Verb) + Object

For example:

He is reading a book.

The passive form of this sentence would be: "A book is being read by him."

Formula:
Active => Sub + M.V (V) + Obj
Passive => Obj + V3 + by + Sub
