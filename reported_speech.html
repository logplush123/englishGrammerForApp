    Some Rules

  1. यदि Reporting Speech मा said to भए told हुन्छ |
    E.g.
    • He said to me, "I can meet you now."
    • He told me that he could meet me then.
    2. यदि Reporting Speech मा says to भए tells हुन्छ |
      E.g.
    • Ravi says to me, "He is my friend."
    • Ravi tells me that he is his friend.
    3. यदि Reporting Speech मा will say to me भए will tell हुन्छ |
      E.g.
    • Ravi will say to me, "He is my friend."
    • Ravi will tell me that he is his friend.
    4. यदि Reporting Speech मा said भए said नै रहन्छ |
      E.g.
    • He said, "I can meet you now."
    • He said that he could meet me then.
    5. यदि Reporting Speech मा says भए says नै रहन्छ |
      E.g.
    • Ravi says, "He is my friend."
    • Ravi says that he is my friend.
    6. यदि Reporting Speech मा will say भए will say नै रहन्छ |
      E.g.
    • Ravi will say, "He is my friend."
    • Ravi will say that he is my friend.
    7. यदि sentences interrogative मा भए said र said to को ठाऊँ मा asked हुन्छ |
      interrogative sentences मा कोटेसन (" ") मार्क्स भित्र को sentences कुनै पनी verb बाट start हुन्छ भने direct बाट indirect मा change गर्दा कोटेसन (" ") मार्क्स हटाइ त्यो ठाऊँ मा if/weather को प्रयोग गरिन्छ |

      verbs => am, is, are, was , were, do, does, did, has, have, had, will, would, shall, should, can, could, may, might, must, etc.

    • Ravi will say, "He is my friend."
    • Ravi will say that he is his friend.
    8. यदि interrogative sentences मा direct बाट indirect मा change गर्दा (" ") मार्क्स भित्र को sentences को सुरुवाद कुनै WH Family member बाट हुन्छ भने त्यो जस्ता को त्यस्तै लेखिन्छ |

Imperative sentences

Imperative sentences ती हुन्छन् जून sentences मा to order, To Request, To Advise गरिन्छ | ती sentences imperative sentences हुन् |
जस्तै :

  1. Order :
    • Go there.
    • Sit here.
  2. Request:
    • Please do not do it.
    • Please do not tell a lie.
  3. Advise:
    • Take this medicine twice a day.
    • change your food habit.

Rules

• यदि imperative sentences direct बाट indirect मा बदल्दा reporting verb (Said/Said to) Reported speech को आधार मा change हुन्छ | Reported speech मा
Ordered = ordered
Request = Requested / urged
Advice = Advised

Reported speech positive मा भए (to) + v1 लाग्छ |

Reported speech Negative मा भए (to) को just अगाडी not + v1 लाग्छ |

Examples

  • The teacher said to Rehman, “stand up right now.”
  • The teacher ordered / instructed Rehman to stand up.
  • Doctor said to the patient, “change your food habits.”
  • Doctor advised / suggested the patient to change his food habits.
  • She said to her husband, “please don’t tell a lie.”
  • She requested / urged her husband not to tell a lie.
  • She said to bobby, “Please give me a glass of water.”
  • She requested / urged to give her a glass of water.
  • Boss said, “follow me.”
  • Boss ordered / instructed to follow him.

Exclamatory Sentences.

Exclamatory Sentences को पहिचान = Expression of Joy, Sorrow, Regret, Applause, or Wonder/surprise.

यदि reported speech मा ख़ुशी, दुःख, पछुतो, आश्चर्य को अभिब्यक्ति हुन्छ भने ती sentences Exclamatory sentences हुन् |

Rule

1. यदि Exclamatory sentences direct बाट indirect मा बदल्दा reporting verb (Said/Said to) Reported speech को आधार मा change हुन्छ | Reported speech मा
Expression of joy = Joy
Expression of sorrow = sorrow
Expression of regret = regret
Expression of applause = applause
Expression of wonder/ surprise = wonder / surprise

2. Exclamatory sentences को अन्त्य मा सधै Exclamation marks [!] चिन्ह लाग्दछ

Examples

  • Ayansh said, “fantastic! We have won the match.”
  • Ayansh exclaimed with joy that they had won the match.
  • She said, “oh! I lost my pen”
  • She exclaimed with sorrow that she had lost pen.
  • Arnavi said, “sorry for being late!”
  • Arnavi exclaimed with regret that she was late.
  • He said, “I can’t believe this!”
  • He exclaimed with wonder / surprise that he could not believe that.
  • My uncle said to me, “Well done! You have done a good job.”
  • My uncle exclaimed with applause that I had done good job.

Optative

Optative Sentences को पहिचान = to pray, Sorrow, wise, bid (अभिवादन) यदि reported speech मा प्राथना, अभिवादन,इच्छा गरिन्छ (हुन्छ) भने ती sentences Optative sentences हुन् |

Rule

• यदि optative sentences direct बाट indirect मा बदल्दा reporting verb (Said/Said to) Reported speech को आधार मा change हुन्छ | Reported speech मा Pray = prayed Bid = bid / wished Wish = wished

Example

  • He said, “good morning, Varun.”
  • He wished Varun good morning.
  • Arohi said to her dad, “Good night.”
  • Arohi bade her dad good night.
  • He said, “would that I were a girl!”
  • He wished that he had been a girl.
  • Aman said, “I wish my friends were here!”
  • Aman wished that his friend had been there.
  • He said to Mayank, “may you success in life!”
  • He prayed that Mayank might succeed in life.
