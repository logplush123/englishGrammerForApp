Yes/No Question

Yes/No question त्यस्तो प्रश्न हो, जसमा प्रश्न गर्दा हो वा होइन भन्ने उत्तर आउँछ।

Is he a doctor ? (के उ चिकित्सक हो ?)
Yes, he is / No, he is not (isn't).

वाक्यमा to be र to have भए त्यसैलाई Subject भन्दा पहिले राखेर प्रश्न बनाइन्छ ।

To be : is, am, are, was, were To have: have, has, had

■ He is a teacher. ( उ शिक्षक हो ।)
✓ Is he a teacher ? ( के उ शिक्षक हो ?)

They have a laptop.
Have they a laptop.

Rule No 1

Form statement into yes no questions
Structure
Auxilary verb + sub ........ main verb+?

Normally :- when there is auxilary verb in the sentences we put in the first to make it yes no questions:
Example:

Aasha is cleaning the room.
was aasha cleaning the room?
She can phone you.
Can she phone you?
I am reading a book.
Am i reading a book?
Rita has been awarded.
Has rita been awarded?

Exercise

We should preserve the jungle.

Correct answer: Should we preserve the jungle?

Ram was playing in the garden.

Correct answer: Was Ram playing in the garden?

They didn't invite you.

Correct answer: Didn't they invite you?

Miss is writing a letter.

Correct answer: Is miss writing a letter?

RULE 2

When there is more then one auxilary varb first one is used to make questions.

Example :-
She will have been doing it.
Will she have been doing it?
Hari has been dancing.
Has hari been dancing?
Seema had been cooking the meal.
Had seema been cooking the meal?
It will have been completed.
Will it have been completed?

Note : In yes/no question ' I ' and ' We ' can use 'shall' while making questions.

rule no . 3

When there is no auxilary verb in the statements we use 'do' while making questions.

Note 'Do' is used in verb1 , 'did' is used in verb2 'Does' is used in verb5, After use 'do' in question we always use varb 1 while making the question.

Example
You invite him.
Do you invite him?
Ramesh plays a ball.
Does ramesh play a ball?
They did it.
Did they did it?
They stopped doing it.
Did they stop diong it?

Rule no. 4

When 'have' is used as a main verb, does is used while making questins. 'Do' is used in 'have' , 'does' is used in 'has' , 'did' is used in

i had a notes.

rule no. 5

'Some' is changed into 'Any' in yes no questions.

Example.
He has bought some books.
has he bought any books?
She told you something.
Did she tell you anything?
Some one is in the room.
is anyone in the room?
Neha went somewhere.
Did neha go anywhere?
