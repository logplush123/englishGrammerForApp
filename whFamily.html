ENGLISH GRAMMAR

Wh Family

WH Questions can also be said as word question.

STRUCTURE

Question word + Auxiliary verb + subject, main verb, + objective part

RULE NO.1

We should remove the answer from the statements.

    E.g.
  • Ram made a car.
  • Who made a car?
  • She has many dogs.
  • Who has many dogs?

RULE NO.2

All the rules of yes/no questions are applied in Wh-questions

Who, Whom, Which, Why, What, how are used in wh questions.

  1. Why :- Why is used for the reason and purpose.

    As :

    • She passed the exam because of her hard study.
    Why did she pass the exam?
    • Ram goes to school to read.
    Why does Ram go to school?
    • They make the coffee to drink.
    Why do they make coffee?
    • You are reading to help her.
    Why are you reading?
  2. Who :- when the person is subject we use 'who' while making questions.

    As :

    • Ram makes food.
    Who makes food?
    • Seema wrote a letter.
    Who wrote a letter?
    • Mother washed the clothes.
    Who washed the clothes?
    • Rita is my best friend.
    Who is my best friend?
  3. What :- Normally 'what' is used in subject verb and object.

    As :

    • He wrote homework.
    What did he write?
    • Ramesh plays ball.
    What does Ramesh play?
    • He had three chocolates
    What did he have?
    • She played a movie.
    What does she do?
  4. Where :- Where is used for the place.

    As :

    • She was born in Dharan.
    Where was she born?
    • Ram went to the office.
    Where did Ram go?
    • Shyam is on the way.
    Where is Shyam?
    • Gita had gone to the market.
    Where had Gita gone?
  5. When :- 'When' is used for the time.

    As :

    • She was born in 2000 AD.
    When was she born?
    • He finished yesterday.
    When did he finish?
    • They got married at the age of 25.
    When did they get married?
    • They will come tomorrow.
    When will they come?
  6. Whose :- Whose shows the relation of a person towards any person or an object.

    As :

    • This is my book.
    Whose book is this?
    • He has borrowed my pen.
    Whose pen has he borrowed?
    • We ate your lunch.
    Whose lunch did we eat?
    • Daddy bought her dress.
    Whose dress did Daddy buy?
  7. How :- How is used for explaining the way of working.

    As :

    • He passed the exam by studying hard.
    How did he pass the exam?
    • I am fine.
    How are you?
    She is not feeling well.
    How is she?
    Sita brings the copy and writes a note.
    How does Sita write a note?
