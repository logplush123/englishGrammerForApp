ENGLISH GRAMMAR GUIDE

Adjectives (विशेषण)

An adjective is a word that modifies, describes, or gives more information about a noun or a pronoun.

(नेपाली: **नाम वा सर्वनामको बारेमा थप जानकारी दिने, त्यसको विशेषता वा गुण बताउने शब्दलाई विशेषण भनिन्छ।**)

१. He is helpful. (यहाँ 'helpful' ले 'He' को विशेषता जनाउँछ)

२. Rahul has a red cap. (यहाँ 'red' ले 'cap' को विशेषता/रंग जनाउँछ)

Types of Adjectives (विशेषणका प्रकार)

  1. १. Adjective of Quality (गुणवाचक विशेषण)

    **Adjectives of quality describe the characteristic, kind, or state of a noun.** (गुण, प्रकार वा अवस्थाको वर्णन गर्दछ।)

    • She is an ugly girl.
    • It’s a beautiful garden.
    • The food is delicious.
    • He is a tall boy.

  2. २. Adjective of Quantity (परिमाणवाचक विशेषण)

    **Adjectives that describe the quantity (how much) of an uncountable noun.** (नगन्न सकिने नामको परिमाणको वर्णन गर्दछ।)

    • I don’t have much milk.
    • You have little rice.
    • Rahul has enough courage.
    • He wants some money.

  3. ३. Adjective of Number (संख्यावाचक विशेषण)

    **Adjectives which indicate the exact number (one, two) or order (first, second), or general number (many, all) of a countable noun.** (गन्न सकिने नामको संख्या वा क्रम जनाउँछ।)

    • He has five marbles.
    • Each girl is dancing.
    • Every word is correct.
    • All men must die.

  4. ४. Demonstrative Adjective (संकेतवाचक विशेषण)

    **Adjectives (This, That, These, Those) that point out a specific person or thing.** (कुनै खास व्यक्ति वा वस्तुलाई संकेत गर्ने विशेषण।)

    • This man is good.
    • That man is weak.
    • These mangoes are sour.

  5. ५. Interrogative Adjective (प्रश्नवाचक विशेषण)

    **Adjectives like "what", "which", and "whose", when used immediately before nouns to ask questions.** (प्रश्न सोध्नको लागि नामभन्दा ठिक अगाडि प्रयोग हुने विशेषण।)

    • Which kind of book do you have?
    • Whose house is this?
    • What pen will you buy?

  6. ६. Possessive Adjective (सम्बन्धवाचक विशेषण)

    **Adjectives (my, your, his, her, its, our, their) that show the state of possession or ownership of the following noun.** (यसले नाममाथि स्वामित्व वा अधिकार देखाउँछ।)

    मुख्य Possessive Adjectives र नेपाली अर्थ:
    **My** (मेरो)
    **Your** (तिम्रो/तपाईंको)
    **His** (उसको - पुरुष)
    **Her** (उनको - महिला)
    **Its** (यसको)
    **Our** (हाम्रो)
    **Their** (उनीहरूको)
    • Where is my notebook?
    • This is our property.
    • His mother is calling.

**Note (ध्यान दिनुहोस्):** Generally, an adjective comes before a noun (**attributive use**), but it can also follow a linking verb (like 'be', 'seem', 'look') (**predicative use**). (सामान्यतया, विशेषण नामभन्दा अगाडि आउँछ, तर यो 'be' जस्ता लिङ्किङ क्रियापछि पनि आउन सक्छ।)
