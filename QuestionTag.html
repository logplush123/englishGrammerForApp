Question Tag

If a short question is added in the sentences, then it is said to be a tag question.

Rule No. 1

In a sense, affirmative sentences are changed into negative, and negative sentences are changed into affirmative.

E.g.

  • Ram is a boy., isn't he?
  • She will not finish her work., will she?
  • We can do it., can’t we?
  • He never worked hard, did he?
  • She cooks the food, doesn’t she?
  • We did not see a party., did we?
  • Rekha teaches her son., doesn’t she?

Exercise

  1. We played football.?



  2. They are singing a song?



  3. Rajan isn't a student?



  4. She can't do it.



Rule No. 2

Auxiliary verb is added along with not in negative tag.

E.g.

  • Ram did his work, didn’t he?
  • Dad drives a car, doesn’t he?
  • My friends drink milk, don’t they?

Exercise

  1. They won't told haven?



  2. Sister washes clothes?



  3. We will dance in a party?



  4. Cathy will call me?



Rule No. 3

Always pronoun is used in the end of the tag.

E.g

  • Ram will play a ball, will not he?
  • Seema has a book, hasn’t she?
  • Roshan helps me, doesn’t he?

Exercise

  1. We shall do the work?


  2. Rama can do her work?


  3. I am a player?


  4. They sing a song?

Rule No 4

In tag question imperative sentences are changed in, will you?

E.g.

  1. Come here, will you?
  2. Have your meal, will you?
  3. Don’t hate him, Will you?

Exercise

  2. Let her go out?


  3. Open the door?


Rule No 5

In tag question Let’s changed into “shall we”

E.g.

  1. Let’s go out, shall we?
  2. Let’s dance, shall we?
  3. Let’s write a note, shall we?

Exercise
